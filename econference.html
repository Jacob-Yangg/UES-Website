ECONference

What is ECONference?

This TED-style conference will consist of multiple panels revolving around topics such as emerging technologies, economic research and its applications, and other related topics. While we have a focus on economics, this conference is open to all majors and we promise everyone will learn something from the event.

We will have speakers coming from LinkedIn and Intensity Corp. to share their feedback and insights in the industry, as well as what they recommend students entering the field. Aside from insightful presentations, ECONference will also include Q&A sections per panelist, as well as networking opportunities. In addition to our speakers, we are also inviting multiple honored guests that are interested in engaging with students. We will have allocated networking time where students can connect with these corporations. This, coupled with free dinner, creates the perfect chance to learn more about experts in the field and what skills they are looking for.

ECONference is on April 16th from 5:30 to 8:30pm in the SSC Multipurpose Room.

Speaker Highlights

Guy Berger


Guy Berger, Ph.D, is the Chief Economist at Linkedin. As Chief Economist, Dr. Berger is responsible for teasing out trends and patterns from Linkedin’s treasure trove of data. His main expertise falls in understanding the labor market, analyzing skill gaps, and analyzing trends in hiring and unemployment. Before Linkedin, Dr. Berger worked as one of the senior economists for the Royal Bank of Scotland and Bank of America’s Corporate Investment Group. Dr. Berger earned his P.h.D in Economics at Yale University, and earned his Bachelors at UC San Diego in Economics and Mathematics.

Ryan Sullivan


Ryan Sullivan, Ph.D. is Chief Executive Officer of Intensity Corporation and provides expertise in economics, finance, and statistics. He applies his expertise to solve complex and challenging issues that organizations face in the competitive marketplace and in the courtroom. Dr. Sullivan has provided professional economic services since 1992. He established Quant Economics, Inc. in 2006 and Intensity Corporation in 2014. Dr. Sullivan earned his B.A., M.A., and Ph.D. in economics from the University of California, San Diego.
